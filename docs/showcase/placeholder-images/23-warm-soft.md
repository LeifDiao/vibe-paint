## Usage Notes
- All images should maintain the warm, cozy aesthetic consistently
- Use terracotta (#E07A5F), sage (#81B29A), and gold (#F2CC8F) as primary accent colors
- Cream (#FFFBF5) or warm off-white backgrounds preferred
- Apply soft watercolor textures for organic feel
- Avoid hard edges; use soft, rounded shapes
- Maintain consistent, warm-tinted soft shadows
- Font: Nunito for any text in images
- No harsh colors or stark contrasts
- All illustrations should feel inviting and welcoming
