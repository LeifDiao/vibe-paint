How It Works
Pick. Paint. Ship.
Three steps. Sixty seconds. A completely new look.
1
Pick a Style
Browse 30 live previews. Every button, card, form, and table — rendered in real time. No guessing. Pick the vibe that fits.
2
Paint
One command. Your AI reads the tokens, scans every file, and replaces every color, font, shadow, and radius across your entire project.
/vibe-paint synthwave
3
Ship
Auto-reviewed for consistency and accessibility. No leftover hardcoded colors. No missed components. Just a cohesive, production-ready UI.
Why vibe-paint
Tokens, Not Adjectives
Other tools say "use warm colors." We give your AI #E8927C.
👁
See It Before You Paint It
Every style is a live, interactive preview — not a screenshot. What you see is exactly what your AI builds.
🎯
200+ Design Tokens Per Style
Colors, typography, radii, shadows, spacing — every value specified to the pixel. No ambiguity. Deterministic results, every time.
⚡
Full-Project Paint
Not a color picker. Not a theme toggle. A full scan-audit-replace-review pipeline that touches every file in your project. Nothing missed.
🌐
Any Framework. Any Stack.
React, Vue, Svelte, Next.js, Tailwind, MUI, plain CSS — it doesn't matter. vibe-paint paints whatever you built, however you built it.
